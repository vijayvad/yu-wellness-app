Privacy Policy Support

1. Information We Collect

Y的 Wellness collects the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and profile information you provide
  • Health Data: With your permission, we access Apple Health data for wellness insights
  • Voice Data: Voice recordings for emotional analysis (processed locally and not stored)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized wellness insights and recommendations
  • Analyze your voice for emotional patterns (processed locally)
  • Integrate with Apple Health for comprehensive wellness tracking
  • Improve our AI algorithms and app functionality

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Voice data is processed locally on your device and never stored permanently
  • Health data is encrypted and stored securely
  • We use Firebase for secure authentication and data storage
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of data collection

6. Health Data Disclaimer

Important: Y的 Wellness is designed for wellness and informational purposes only. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult with healthcare professionals for medical advice.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
